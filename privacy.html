Privacy Protocol

01. Asset Confidentiality

At Editzaar, we understand the value of your raw intellectual property. All footage, scripts, and brand assets provided for Finance, Real Estate, or Education projects are stored on secure, encrypted drives. We never share raw files with third parties.

02. Data Collection

We only collect essential information (Name, Email, Project Briefs) required to facilitate the editing process. Your contact data is strictly used for project communication and is never sold to marketing lists.

03. Portfolio Usage

We respect "First Release" rights. Editzaar will only showcase edited clips in our agency portfolio after the client has publicly released the content, or with explicit written consent.

04. Contact

For any inquiries regarding your data or asset security, reach out directly to our lead editor at editzaarbooking@gmail.com.
